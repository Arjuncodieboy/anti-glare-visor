An optical solution for headlight glare.

This is a clip-on visor designed to reduce harsh headlight glare without dimming the rest of the scene. It selectively manages incoming light so contrast, depth, and clarity are preserved.

Built for people who ride or drive at night and experience eye strain, irritation, or reduced comfort due to modern headlights.

The problem has changed.

Headlights today are brighter, higher mounted, and more focused than before. While this improves forward visibility, it also produces intense glare for oncoming riders and drivers.

The result is not darkness, but unmanaged light entering the eye — making it harder to focus, uncomfortable to look ahead, and tiring over time.

A different approach.

Instead of reducing brightness across the entire view, this visor reshapes how specific glare reaches the eye. Optical filtering softens high-intensity sources while keeping the surrounding scene readable.

The system is fully passive. No electronics, no processing delay, and no loss of situational awareness.
