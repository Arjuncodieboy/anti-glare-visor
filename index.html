An optical solution for headlight glare.

A clip-on visor designed to reduce headlight glare without darkening vision, allowing drivers and riders to see clearly even in harsh lighting conditions. Instead of dimming the entire scene, it focuses on managing how light reaches the eye.

The goal is simple: preserve contrast, reduce eye strain, and make night driving or riding more comfortable without changing how you normally see the road.

Headlight glare has changed.

Modern headlights are brighter, higher mounted, and far harsher than older lighting systems. While this improves visibility for some, it often results in intense glare for others, especially in oncoming traffic or poorly lit environments.

For many night riders and drivers, this constant exposure to bright, unfocused light makes driving tiring, irritating to the eyes, and visually uncomfortable over time. The problem isn’t darkness — it’s unmanaged light.

A different way to deal with glare.

Instead of reducing all incoming light, this approach uses optical filtering to selectively soften harsh glare while keeping the rest of the scene clear and readable. This helps maintain depth, contrast, and situational awareness.

The design is fully passive, meaning there are no electronics, no power requirements, and no delay. The optical effect is always active and adapts naturally to changing lighting conditions.
