An optical solution for headlight glare.

A clip-on visor designed to reduce harsh headlight glare without darkening your vision. It selectively manages incoming light so the scene remains clear, balanced, and comfortable to view.

Built for night riders and drivers who experience fatigue, irritation, and visual discomfort from modern headlights.

Headlight glare has changed.

Modern headlights are brighter, higher mounted, and harsher than before. While they improve visibility for some, they often create unmanaged glare for oncoming riders and drivers.

This glare makes it harder to focus, strains the eyes, and turns night travel into a tiring and irritating experience. The issue isn’t darkness — it’s uncontrolled light entering the eye.

A different way to deal with glare.

Instead of dimming everything, this approach reshapes how light reaches the eye. Optical filtering softens intense glare while keeping the rest of the scene readable and natural.

The design is fully passive — no electronics, no processing delay, and no compromise to situational awareness.
