Headlight glare has changed.

Modern headlights are brighter, higher mounted, and harsher than before. The result is not better vision, but frequent glare and eye strain.

For many night riders and drivers, this makes riding or driving tiring, irritating, and visually uncomfortable.

The problem isn’t darkness. It’s unmanaged light.

A different way to deal with glare.

Instead of dimming everything, this approach reshapes how light reaches the eye.

Optical filtering softens harsh glare while keeping the rest of the scene balanced and readable — avoiding the trade-offs of tinted solutions.

The design is fully passive. No electronics. No delay.
