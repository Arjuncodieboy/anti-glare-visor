An optical solution for headlight glare.

A clip-on visor designed to reduce headlight glare without darkening your vision.

This product is a compact optical element that selectively manages incoming light, preserving clarity, contrast, and depth even in challenging night conditions.

  • Passive optical design
  • No electronics or power
  • Built to integrate with what you already use

Headlight glare has become unavoidable.

Modern headlights are brighter, higher mounted, and harsher than before. While visibility has improved on paper, glare and visual fatigue have increased in practice.

For many night riders and drivers, glare makes it harder to ride or drive comfortably, causing constant eye strain and irritation — not because of darkness, but because of excess, unmanaged light.

The issue isn’t brightness alone. It’s how light interacts with human vision.

A different way to deal with glare.

Instead of reducing all incoming light, this approach focuses on managing it.

Optical filtering softens harsh headlight glare while keeping the rest of the scene clear and balanced, avoiding the visibility loss common with tinted solutions.

The design is fully passive — no electronics, no delay — always active as lighting conditions change.
